Crystal Collector

How to Play!

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

It's up to you to find the value of each crystal.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change what each crystal is worth.
